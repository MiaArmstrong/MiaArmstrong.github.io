About Mia Armstrong

Hello and welcome! I'm Mia Armstrong, a seasoned Senior Software Quality Assurance Engineer with a passion for automation and a track record of excellence. With over 8 years of experience, I've had the privilege of leading QA projects across a spectrum of industries, from healthcare and finance to entertainment and grocery delivery startups.


Why Automation?

Automation is my forte, and I take pride in crafting modular, reusable automation suites that not only streamline testing but also elevate the quality of software products. My expertise spans a range of programming languages, including TypeScript, Javascript, Java, and C#, along with a deep understanding of tools like Selenium, Playwright, and Cypress.


Beyond Testing

While my heart belongs to quality assurance, I'm not confined to it. I'm an advocate for continuous improvement and learning. I've dabbled in a multitude of technologies, from AWS Elastic Beanstalk to Swagger, always eager to expand my skill set and tackle new challenges.


Remote Work Enthusiast

Remote work has been a significant part of my journey, allowing me to collaborate with global teams and adapt to diverse work environments. It's a testament to my adaptability and commitment to delivering results, no matter where in the world I'm located.


Let's Connect

I'm excited to share my experiences and expertise with you through this portfolio. Whether you're looking to collaborate, seeking advice, or simply want to discuss the latest developments in software quality assurance, don't hesitate to get in touch. Let's connect and explore the possibilities together!

Thank you for visiting my portfolio. Let's embark on this journey of quality and innovation together.
