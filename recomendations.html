1. Recomendation from Joshua B., QA Manager 2023

When I hired Mia, I knew that she had an outstanding background and that she would be an excellent fit on the team, even if she didn't see it yet. What I did not know was that the project I would be putting her on would be an incredibly frustrating and protracted adventure of shifting scopes, third-party integrations that absolutely did not work how they were supposed to, and questionable technical decisions. Nevertheless, Mia did her job extremely patiently and extremely well, going far beyond anyone's reasonable expectations and dramatically improving what had been very shaky confidence in the project's ability to complete, much less complete as expected or anywhere near on time. While she had come to me as a seasoned professional, she came out on the other side of that project as a well recognized authority on quality in general and the many, many intricacies that can arise in the complex interactions of many, many backend services. While I hope that Mia never again has to work on a project like that one, I have an unshakable faith in her ability to truly own quality for projects and products of any size, advocating cogently for the teams she supports to make sensible, reasonable, humane decisions that balance business needs with the experience of the customer, and providing thorough, careful automated testing regimes to ensure that customer experience. I would not hesitate to invite Mia to work with me again.
